Chicken Pepperoni

Savor the irresistible combination of tender chicken and perfectly spiced pepperoni, layered with rich, melted cheese and our signature tomato sauce. This dish is a crowd-pleaser, perfect for those who crave a hearty and satisfying meal. Pair it with your favorite sides and enjoy an unforgettable dining experience.
